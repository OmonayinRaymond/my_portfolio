About Me

Hi, I'm Raymond, a web developer with a passion for storytelling through design. My journey began in high school when I built a website for a local charity. That project sparked my love for web development.

Today, I help businesses create digital experiences that make an impact. I listen to your goals and craft solutions that truly connect with your audience.

My Skills:

  • HTML & CSS: Creating stylish and engaging websites.
  • JavaScript & React: Bringing interactivity to enhance user experiences.
  • UI/UX Design: Ensuring seamless and user-friendly journeys.

I love working with passionate individuals. To me, your website is more than just an online presence; it reflects your values and vision.

Outside of coding, I enjoy making friends and discovering new music.

Let's work together to turn your vision into reality. I'm excited to help you create something amazing!
